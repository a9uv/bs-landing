Early Stage Growth

We specialize in strategizing an optimal funding plan for Startups and Early Stage Growth Organizations that must balance the need for financing with preserving ownership and avoiding dilution. We value and support your organization to achieve a judicious equity distribution that provides liquidity and enables your organization to reach more favorable market valuations. Though pre-revenue and pre-cashflow organizations are often unable to access traditional debt markets, our team helps you access funding plans that give you the runway you need to take off:

Venture Leasing: Our venture leasing services allow your start-up to acquire operating equipment while reducing the impact on venture development capital. Asset classes include technology, transportation, manufacturing & production, material handling, and most other "hard" assets.

Structure Finance: This service will enable you to to monetize assets and cashflow, opening up access to funding that is otherwise inaccessible.

Commercial Partners: We provide start-ups like yours access to relationships with larger businesses to create liquidity through credit-enhancement, subleases, and other commercial arrangements.
